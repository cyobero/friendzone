    About FriendZone

    Mission: To establish an open community in which users can solicit helpful, unbiased dating and relationship advice from all walks of life.

    To this end, we aim to keep the site simple by keeping features at a minimum. The three core features that make up FriendZone are:

    1. Allowing users to register for an account and sign in.
    2. Once they have an account, allow them to ask any dating or relationship advice they so choose.
    3. Allow other users to answer any question they wish to answer.

    If a feature does not fall under one of the above listed categories, we typically do not add it. But, of course, never say never.

    Dating and relationships can sometimes feel more complicated than rocket science. Our aim at FriendZone is to establish an open community in which we can make the art (and sometimes science) of dating and relationships more manageable and hopefully easier to understand through the accumulation and sharing of knowledge. We try to let the users moderate themselves and only intervene when a user's actions, comments, or behavior is highly inappropriate. At FriendZone, we are advocates of free speech, and let people express themselves however they choose to express themselves.That being said, expect a few trolls on some of the posts you might come across on FriendZone. We let the trolls be trolls, and we will only prevent them from making comments if it reaches a point of any form of harassment, or if law enforcement has to get involved.
