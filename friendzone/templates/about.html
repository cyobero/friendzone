    About FriendZone

    Mission: To establish an open community in which users can solicit helpful, unbiased dating and relationship advice from all walks of life.

    Dating and relationships can sometimes feel more complicated than rocket science. The aim at FriendZone is to establish an open community in which we can make the art (and sometimes science) of dating and relationships more manageable and hopefully easier to understand through the accumulation and sharing of knowledge. We let users moderate themselves and only intervene when a user's actions, comments, or behavior is highly inappropriate. At FriendZone, we are advocates of free speech, and let people express themselves however they choose to express themselves.We will only prevent them from making comments if it reaches a point of any form of harassment, or if law enforcement has to get involved.
